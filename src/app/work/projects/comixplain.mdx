# Explaining through Comics

**Project Homepage:** [https://fhstp.github.io/comixplain/](https://fhstp.github.io/comixplain/)

Making complex science accessible through storytelling. Designed educational comics that engage students with diverse learning styles and knowledge levels, transforming abstract concepts into compelling visual narratives.

### The Challenge



### My Role & Responsibilities
- Conducting user research, usability testing, interviews, workshops with educators and students
- Development of comics story and general concept 

### Approach & Strategy
We propose Comixplain, a set of educational comics and supporting materials designed to make STEM concepts more accessible, engaging, and adaptable to diverse teaching contexts based on a visual storytelling-based strategy.

### Key Complexities Addressed
- Make STEM concepts and topics more accessible and engaging
- Make the Comics adaptable for diverse teaching contexts and different educators
- Find real-world-based metaphors for the comics the students can relate to

### Outcomes & Impact
- Five Comics [https://fhstp.github.io/comixplain/](https://fhstp.github.io/comixplain/)
- User guide support lecturers and instructors to better understand comiccs and how to create them
- Modular template allowing the use of whole stories or a fewer set of panels according to the needs of educators


**Technologies/Methods:** User-centered design, workshops
